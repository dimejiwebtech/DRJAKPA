Institutional Medical Migration

Built by Doctors
For Doctors.

We replace structural chaos with a definitive roadmap. dr.Jakpa is the institutional bridge between African medical talent and the German healthcare system.

Our Core Intent

Germany faces a massive shortage of medical professionals. Nigeria possesses a surplus of overworked, underpaid talent. The bridge between them is currently broken by misinformation and predatory agents.

Our agency helps Nigerian doctors migrate to Germany legally by focusing on the four pillars that matter: Licensing, Language, Employment, and Relocation.

The Kharkiv-Berlin Journey

Dr. Jakpa was founded by a doctor who lived through the structural chaos.

The Struggle

Studying in Kharkiv, Ukraine, and fleeing to Germany after the war only to find degrees academically valid but professionally invisible.

The Breakthrough

Navigating the specific path from Nigeria back to Germany, passing TELC B2 and FSP exams without a mentor or community.

The Purpose

Helping others avoid the isolation and wasted years by providing a responsible bridge—showing exactly where the road is broken.
